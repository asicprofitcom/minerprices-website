How This Works

MinerPrices displays live profitability rankings for ASIC miners. Data includes real miner specs from top manufacturers and profitability is calculated using current cryptocurrency prices.

  • 💰 Daily Profit = Mining revenue (at current prices) - Electricity cost ($0.08/kWh)
  • ⛏️ All major ASIC miners from Bitmain, MicroBT, Whatsminer included
  • 📈 Prices update every 5 minutes automatically
  • 🔗 Data from live market prices (CoinGecko)
